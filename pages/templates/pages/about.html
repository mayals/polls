About CrowdVoice Polls
CrowdVoice Polls: Understand Your Audience

CROWDVOICE Polls is a web application built with Python, Django web framework, empowering you to participate in and shape engaging polls. We believe in the collective power of voices shaping opinions and driving change.

Core Features


  • ✔ Create Polls (Owner role)
  • ✔ Vote on Polls (Voter & Owner roles)
  • ✔ Real-time Vote Results
  • ✔ User Management (Voter & Owner roles)
  • ✔ Content Moderation (Admin)

Our Mission

CROWDVOICE Polls strives to provide a platform that:

  • Empowers individuals to have their voices heard.
  • Fosters informed decision-making through accessible polls.
  • Promotes open communication and the exchange of diverse viewpoints.

Our Values

We operate with the following core values guiding our development and interactions:

  • Transparency: Clear and open communication.
  • Inclusivity: A welcoming environment for everyone.
  • Integrity: Responsible and ethical platform usage.
